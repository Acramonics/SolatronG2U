Centronics Connector
====================

They are known as '57 Series' connectors, SCSI connectors (though
there are other connectors also called SCSI connectors), or Centronics
connectors, (or occasionally IEEE-488 connectors!)

These can be a bit difficult to find in the PDB-mounting straight
version. Solder-cup versions are easy to obtain as are the
right-angled versions.  The PCB solder-pin version is sometimes
described as panel mount (rather than line mount for solder-cup).

NorComp seems to be the most common manufacturer and you want the
'111' series for straight connectors ('112' series is right-angle
while '211/212' are the female versions).  Specifically, the part
number you want for a straight connector is `111-024-113L001`.

Currently (April, 2026) in the UK they are available from Mouser,
Farnell (special order) and DigiKey

A much cheaper equivalent is available from AliExpress, but they are
hard to track down amongst all the right-angled and solder-cup
versions. I found them on the 'SKYWIN Store' (Store no.:1102628842) at
https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005005636771892.html Select 'Panel
Type', 24P, Male.
